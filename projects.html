Projects

Evolution of meso-structures and mechanical properties of granular materials under triaxial compression state from complex network perspective

Aiming at contributing to the establishment of the multi-scale constitutive model. The evolution of connectivity of granular materials with different initial densities was investigated from the complex network perspective.

Mechanics tests of mudstone in Tianshui area

Focus on the basic mechanical characteristics of the rock samples of typical soft rock slopes in Tianshui area, including: bulk density, water absorption rate, uniaxial compressive strength (natural and saturated), modulus of elasticity and Poisson ratio, acoustic wave test, straight shear strength test, expansion test.

Ashtabula Harbor Project

This project aid in establishing an improved design for the proposed wetland along the breakwater in the Ashtabula Harbor.
