Mechanics tests of mudstone in Tianshui area

Introduction

In order to combine the investigation and research of typical landslides in Tianshui area, basic mechanical tests in Anyuan Town and its surrounding areas must be carried out in order to further reveal the disaster mechanism of this kind of soft rock landslides.

In this project, the basic mechanical characteristics of the rock samples of typical soft rock slopes in Tianshui area were carried out. Including: bulk density, water absorption rate, uniaxial compressive strength (natural and saturated), modulus of elasticity and Poisson ratio, acoustic wave test, straight shear strength test, expansion test.
